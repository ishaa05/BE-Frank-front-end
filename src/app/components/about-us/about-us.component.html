Vidyadaan Sahayyak Mandal

Vidyadaan Sahayyak Mandal, Thane (VSM Thane) is a non-profit organization founded in 2008 to support needy and deserving students in fulfilling their dream of higher education by providing emotional and professional support, rather than just sponsoring academic fees.

VSM has developed its own grooming model where personality development and social awareness are equally important as academic and professional development. We aim to create a sense of community and connection while helping students develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Our Initiatives

One of our most popular initiatives is the 'Be Frank' program, which started in 2013 as a free and frank interaction with students of VSM Hostel and has since grown into a program focused on fostering a progressive mindset in students.

School Chale Hum Program

The 'School Chale Hum' program, led by the Be Frank team, is specially designed for school children from weaker sections of society. Through over 70 VSM sessions, 30+ school sessions, and 10+ special event sessions, we have reached more than 2,000 students. This activity-based learning program focuses on developing self-confidence, public speaking, social skills, and a progressive mindset in students.
